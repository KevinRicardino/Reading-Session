# 🪶 Reading Session Controller

Welcome to the official repository of my Reading Session Controller! This is a responsive, distraction-free, and interactive web layout built to organize reading lists, manage reader orders, and switch interface themes dynamically.

👉 **Check out the live application here:** [https://kevinricardino.github.io/Reading-Session/](https://kevinricardino.github.io/Reading-Session/)

---

### ❤️ Why I Created This Project

Books have a magical way of bringing people together, and **I am absolutely passionate about reading**. Getting lost in stories, discovering new vocabulary, and sharing those moments with others is one of my favorite things in the world. 

Because of this passion, I am constantly hosting casual reading sessions on **Discord** with my friends. We read all kinds of books together, practicing our English in an environment with no judgments and no strict rules. 

I created this tool because I wanted a beautiful, clean, and fluid interface to stream on Discord during our sessions—something that could adapt perfectly to the corner of a screen and keep our reading flow organized. **This project was made purely and simply for the love of reading and having fun!** 🎈✨

### 💡 Core Features

* **Dynamic Reading Order:** Add or remove readers instantly on the go.
* **Smart Navigation:** Pressing `ENTER` on any line automatically moves the cursor to the next reader or spawns a new slot if it's the last one.
* **Faint Placeholders:** Clean inputs that hide helper texts the moment you click to type.
* **Eye-Comfort Themes:** Toggle effortlessly between Sepia 📜, Minimalist White ☀️, and Midnight Black 🌙.
* **Ultra-Responsive Design:** Tailored to fit perfectly even when shrunk to small window sizes or snapped side-by-side with a book or chat.
